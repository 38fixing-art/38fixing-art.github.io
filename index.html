38Fixers — Empowering Women Through Repair

38Fixers（强记女工）— 以维修赋能女性

We provide practical repair training, on-site installation and maintenance services, and create employment opportunities for women in a male-dominated industry. We publicly commit to reinvesting at least 51% of our annual surplus into our mission.

我们提供实用维修培训、上门安装与维护服务，并为处于男性主导行业的女性创造就业机会。我们公开承诺将每年至少 51% 的盈余投入企业使命。

Public Commitment

社会责任承诺

38Fixers publicly commits to reinvesting 51% or more of annual surplus into social mission activities such as women’s technical training, community initiatives, and sustainable repair services. This commitment will be embedded into our governing documents.

38Fixers 在此公开承诺：每年将 至少 51% 的盈余重新投入企业社会使命（如女性技术培训、社区项目和可持续维修服务），并会将此承诺写入公司治理文件。
